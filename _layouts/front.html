News update Sept 2: SummitStone Health will join us on site. 

News update: August 29 Ashely Noack will be doing a hands on workshop where attendees will get to make their own herbal remedies and take them home to treat anxiety and depression.

The Mental Health Festival aims to challenge perceptions, support art, and introduce tools and resources, all the while entertaining with cutting edge programing. The experience will take place through workshops, films, live music, comedy and more. Slated for October 14 & 15, events will occur at various venues in Fort Collins.
